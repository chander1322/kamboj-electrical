Kaafi log yeh nahi jaante ki ghar ki electrical wiring bhi ek lifetime hoti hai — jaise gaadi ki battery ya chhat ki sheet. Purani wiring na sirf current waste karti hai, balki fire ka khatra bhi ban sakti hai.

![Purani vs Nayi Wiring](/images/wires.png)

## Wiring Ki Average Life Kitni Hoti Hai?

| Wiring Ka Type | Life |
|---|---|
| PVC wire (normal) | 15–20 saal |
| FRLS wire (fire resistant) | 25–30 saal |
| Aluminum wiring (purani) | 10–15 saal |

Agar tumhara ghar **2005 se pehle** bana hai aur wiring kabhi change nahi hui — **check karwao zaroor**.

## 5 Signs Ki Wiring Change Karni Chahiye

### 1. Switch/Socket Garam Ho Raha Hai
Normal use mein switch ya socket touch karne pe thoda bhi garam lage — yeh warning sign hai.

### 2. MCB Baar Baar Trip Karta Hai
Monthly ek-do baar trip ho toh theek, lekin **roz ya hafte mein trip** ho toh wiring overloaded hai.

### 3. Plug Lagate Waqt Spark Aaye
Plug insert karte time spark aana normal nahi hai — yeh loose connection ya damaged socket ki wajah se hota hai.

### 4. Bijli Ka Bill Achanak Badh Gaya
Purani wiring mein **leakage current** hoti hai — yeh dikh nahi ti lekin bill mein zaroor dikhti hai.

### 5. Wire Ka Plastic Peela/Brittle Ho Gaya
Wiring expose ho toh dekho — **yellow, cracked ya brittle plastic** matlab wire ki life khatam ho gayi hai.

## Kya Khud Check Kar Sakte Hain?

Haan! Yeh basic checks khud kar sakte ho:

- **Switchboards dekho** — kala rang (burning marks) dikhe toh sign hai
- **MCB panel check karo** — loose wires ya rust nahi honi chahiye
- **Smell check** — plastic jalne ki smell aaye toh turant switch off karo

> ⚠️ **Important:** Wiring ke andar ka kaam — cable replace karna, joints banana — hamesha certified electrician se hi karwao. DIY wiring se fire ka risk hota hai.
