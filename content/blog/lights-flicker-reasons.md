Pichle hafte ek customer ka call aaya — ghar ki lights baar-baar flicker ho rahi thi, aur unki wife ne bola "ye ghost hai shayad!" 😄 Asal mein wajah ekdum simple thi — **loose neutral connection**. Aaj baat karte hain ki lights flicker karne ke asli reasons kya hote hain.

## 1. Loose Wiring Connection
Sabse common reason. Switch board ya panel mein connection dheela ho jaata hai, jisse current flow unstable ho jaata hai.

## 2. Overloaded Circuit
Agar ek hi circuit pe AC, fridge, aur heavy appliances chal rahe hain, to voltage drop hota hai aur lights flicker karti hain.

## 3. Faulty Bulb ya Holder
Kabhi-kabhi problem bulb mein hi hoti hai, poore ghar ki wiring mein nahi.

## 4. Voltage Fluctuation (Grid Side)
Agar pure mohalle mein flickering ho rahi hai, to ye aapke ghar ki problem nahi, electricity board ki supply mein fluctuation ho sakta hai.

## 5. Damaged Switch
Purana ya damaged switch bhi loose contact create karta hai.

## Kab Khud Fix Karein, Kab Electrician Bulayein?

Agar sirf **ek bulb** flicker kar raha hai → bulb change karke check kar lo, khud handle ho sakta hai.

Agar **poore ghar** ki lights flicker kar rahi hain, ya **sparking/jalne ki smell** aaye → **turant electrician bulayein**, ye safety issue hai.
